Our story

The Complete Package.

Cyclic Agency is one of the very few companies in Romania that can deliver a truly complete event — venue, professional equipment and internationally recognized DJs, all from a single team that has been doing this for 17 years. Whether it's a corporate event for a hundred guests or a night that fills a hall with thousands, the infrastructure and expertise are already in place.

That position was earned, not given. Ionuț Hupcă started organizing parties in Focșani before there was a business plan — before there was even a name. His first venture, Electro Events, was a two-day outdoor festival that lost money. He continued anyway. By 2009, Cyclic Agency existed as a conviction: Romanian audiences deserved the same quality of electronic music experiences as Berlin, Amsterdam or London.

Over 17 years, that conviction turned into one of Romania's most established event names — bringing Sasha, Dixon, Tale of Us, Amelie Lens and dozens of world-class artists to stages across 30 cities. The reputation wasn't built on the marquee bookings. It was built on consistency: every event organized correctly, from first contact to last beat, without exception.

The same standard shaped Cyclic Rentals. What started as internal necessity — having the right gear for their own events — grew into one of Romania's recognized names in professional equipment rental. Smaller promoters and companies across the country who need access to pro-grade sound, lighting and staging now rely on Cyclic. The belief behind it has never changed: quality production should not be a privilege of large budgets.

Alongside the events and rentals, Cyclic has always been a nursery for Romanian talent — discovering artists, managing careers, and pushing them onto international stages. Optick, Rosario Internullo, Mihai Popoviciu, Adrian Eftimie, The Model.

Today, Cyclic also produces The Bohemian Society — one of Romania's most original festivals, at its 4th edition in 2026. Art, electronic music and community in the Danube Delta. It defines exactly what Cyclic has always stood for.

"Profitable is only if you do very large events. We operate in the middle zone — and that's exactly where the trust is built."

— Ionuț Hupcă, Director of Events

Milestones

17 Years in Numbers

2009

The Beginning — Focșani

Before Cyclic had a name, there was Electro Events — a two-day outdoor electronic music festival in Focșani. The first edition lost money. The audience's reaction made it impossible to stop. Cyclic Agency is born from that stubbornness.

2012

First International Headliners

Cyclic begins booking major international names — Sasha among the first — establishing the agency as the bridge between Romania's passionate underground audience and the global electronic music circuit.

2015

Expansion to 10 Cities

Cyclic extends beyond Bucharest, producing events in Cluj-Napoca, Timișoara, Brașov and 6 more cities. Events move to rooftops, industrial spaces and unconventional venues — a signature that defines the brand to this day.

2019

100th Event & Cyclic Records

We celebrate our 100th event with a special Bucharest edition. Cyclic Records launches under the direction of Mihai Popoviciu — giving the roster's artists a home for their recorded music and completing the loop from discovery to release.

2023

The Bohemian Society — Edition I

Cyclic launches The Bohemian Society, one of Romania's most original festivals — a blend of electronic music, art and community set in unique outdoor locations. The first edition sets a new standard for what a Romanian festival can be.

2026

30 Cities, 100+ Artists & The Bohemian Society

Cyclic Agency operates across 30 Romanian cities with a roster of over 100 artists. And now, one of the most exciting festivals in Romania — The Bohemian Society — where art, electronic music and community come together exactly as Cyclic always intended.

What drives us

Our Values

Music First

Every decision starts with the music. We book artists we believe in. We design events around the sonic experience. Commercial considerations come second.

Community

Electronic music is nothing without its people. We build communities, support local artists, and create spaces where culture can genuinely grow.

Authenticity

We don't chase trends. We stay true to underground music culture — its values, its aesthetics, its refusal to compromise for the mainstream.

Craft

From sound design to visual presentation, every element of our events is approached with care and expertise earned through 17 years of experience.

Global Perspective

We connect Romania's scene with the international electronic music world — bringing the best here while pushing Romanian talent beyond our borders.

Growth

We invest in the long-term. In developing artists, in building venues, in growing audiences. Sustainable growth over quick wins, always.
