Frequently asked

Sound FAQ.

Which sound systems do you rent out?
Our main inventory is Void Acoustics — Air line array (Air Ten, Air Vintage), Arcline 218/118 subwoofers and Q5/V9 amplifiers. For large main stages we also bring in d&b audiotechnik or L-Acoustics through sub-rental. We configure the system around the venue (club, warehouse, open-air) and the capacity (from 100 to 5,000+ people).
What is the largest capacity your system can cover?
We configure for events from 100 to 5,000+ people. For larger capacities we sub-rent from vetted partners. SPL and coverage are sized per venue, based on measurements and software prediction.
Is a FOH engineer included in the quote?
Yes. The standard package includes a FOH engineer and, on request, a monitor engineer for complex riders. For dry hire (equipment only) we also offer phone support for clients with their own technical crew.
Do you work outdoor events and festivals?
Yes. We have inventory sized for open-air and experience in demanding locations — we produced The Bohemian Society in the Danube Delta, rooftop events and industrial clubs. Cardioid subwoofers for outdoor acoustic control.
Which areas do you cover?
All of Romania. We run events regularly in 22 cities — Bucharest, Cluj, Timișoara, Iași, Constanța, Focșani and remote locations. Equipment transport is included in the quote.
