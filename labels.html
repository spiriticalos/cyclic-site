Music labels

Labels.

The record labels shaping Romania's underground electronic music scene. From homegrown independents to internationally distributed imprints.

Our role

Connecting Labels to
Audiences.

Cyclic Agency has long been a bridge between Romania's most exciting underground labels and the audiences hungry for quality electronic music. We work with labels at every stage — from release parties and label nights to artist booking and brand partnerships.

Our network spans 30 Romanian cities and extends internationally, giving labels the platform to reach new audiences and grow their communities beyond the digital space.

Partner labels

Labels We Work With

Cyclic Records

Techno House

The in-house imprint of Cyclic Agency, releasing music from Romania's finest underground artists. From deep techno to melodic house — authentic electronic music rooted in the Romanian scene.

Arpiar

Techno

Bucharest-based techno imprint known for its raw, industrial aesthetic and uncompromising releases. Home to some of Romania's most respected techno artists and producers.

Sound of Cyclic

Melodic House

A sublabel dedicated to melodic and organic house music. Releases from Romanian and international artists who blur the lines between electronic and acoustic worlds.

Darker Waves

Dark Techno

Underground Romania's home for darker, experimental techno. Digital and vinyl releases from emerging and established artists pushing the boundaries of the genre.

Cluj Groove

House Disco

Cluj-Napoca's premier house and disco label, celebrating the city's vibrant club culture. Monthly releases featuring local talent and occasional international collaborations.

Tropic Bass

Afro House Global Bass

Bridging Romanian club culture with global rhythms. A forward-thinking label releasing afro house, global bass and Afro-fusion electronic music with a distinctly Romanian perspective.

Label partnerships

How We Support Labels

Artist Development

We work closely with labels to develop their artists' live careers — from first club bookings to major festival slots.

Event Marketing

Integrated marketing support for label nights and release events — social media, press, partnerships and on-the-ground promotion.

International Reach

Our network extends beyond Romania. We help labels connect with international booking agencies, promoters and press.

Venue Access

Established relationships with Romania's top venues mean labels get access to spaces that suit their aesthetic and capacity needs.

Production Support

Full technical production for label events — sound, lighting and visual design that matches and elevates the label's brand identity.

Brand Partnerships

We facilitate brand collaborations and sponsorships that make sense for underground labels — authentic, non-intrusive and community-driven.
