Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CYCLIC MUSIC S.R.L. ("Cyclic", "we", "us" or "our") collects, uses and protects personal data when you visit cyclic.ro. We are committed to processing personal data in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and Romanian Law 190/2018.

1. Data Controller

CYCLIC MUSIC S.R.L.

Registered office: Str. Fabrica de Chibrituri 24-26, Sector 5, București, 050183, Romania

Fiscal code (CUI): 36792785

Trade Registry: J40/15762/2016

Email: ionut@cyclic.ro

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data needed to operate this website and respond to your contact.

  • Newsletter subscription — your email address, when you submit it via the newsletter form.
  • Direct contact — the information you choose to include when you email us, message us on WhatsApp, or call us using the contact details published on this site.
  • Technical logs — our hosting provider (Vercel) automatically records standard server logs (IP address, browser type, time of request) for security, abuse prevention and uptime monitoring. These logs are retained by the provider for a short period and are not used by us for tracking or profiling.
  • Local storage — see our Cookie Policy for details on the small amount of data stored in your browser.

We do not use analytics, advertising trackers, social-media pixels or any third-party tracking technologies on this website.

3. Why We Collect It (Purposes)

  • To send the newsletter you signed up for, including event announcements, early access and exclusive offers.
  • To respond to your inquiries about events, artist bookings, label partnerships, production rentals or career applications.
  • To keep the website available and secure via standard hosting logs.

4. Legal Basis

  • Consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)) — for newsletter subscriptions. You can withdraw at any time using the unsubscribe link in every email or by emailing us.
  • Legitimate interest (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)) — for replying to your direct inquiries and for keeping the site secure and operational.
  • Legal obligation (GDPR Art. 6(1)(c)) — when we have to retain records to comply with tax, accounting or regulatory requirements.

5. How Long We Keep It

  • Newsletter email — until you unsubscribe, or after 24 months of inactivity, whichever comes first.
  • Inquiry correspondence — for as long as needed to resolve the matter, then up to 12 months for reference.
  • Server logs — retained by our hosting provider according to their standard retention.

6. Who We Share It With

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Limited access is granted only to:

  • Vercel Inc. — our hosting provider, which stores and serves the website.
  • Email infrastructure providers — when we send newsletters or replies (e.g. mail relay services).
  • Romanian authorities — only where we are legally required to do so.

External links on this site (for example to Kompostor for ticket purchases, or to WhatsApp / social-media platforms) are governed by the privacy policies of those third parties.

7. International Transfers

Some of our service providers (such as Vercel) may process data outside the European Economic Area. Where this happens, transfers are protected by Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent safeguards approved by the European Commission.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erase your data ("right to be forgotten") where the conditions are met.
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Romanian supervisory authority, ANSPDCP (dataprotection.ro).

To exercise any of these rights, email us at ionut@cyclic.ro. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Automated Decision-Making

We do not carry out automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you.

10. Children

This website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be flagged on this page, with the updated date shown above.

12. Contact
