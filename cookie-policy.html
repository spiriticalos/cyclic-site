Cookie Policy

Last updated: 22 May 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how CYCLIC MUSIC S.R.L. uses cookies and similar browser-storage technologies on cyclic.ro. We take a deliberately minimal approach.

1. What are cookies and local storage?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. Local storage is a similar mechanism built into modern browsers that allows a site to save small pieces of information (such as a preference) directly in your browser, without sending it back to the server on every request.

Both are widely used to remember user preferences, keep sessions active, or measure how a site is used.

2. Consent categories

When you first visit the Site, our cookie banner offers two categories:

  • Strictly necessary — required for the Site to function. Cannot be turned off. Set automatically when you use the Site.
  • Analytics — helps us understand how visitors use the Site so we can improve it. Off by default; loaded only after you opt in via the banner.

You can change your choice at any time by clicking Cookie Settings in the footer of any page, or by clearing your browser storage.

3. Strictly necessary storage

We do not set HTTP cookies in this category. We use a very small number of localStorage entries, all required for the user-interface to behave correctly. They contain no identifying information.

Key Purpose Duration
cookie_consent_v2 Records your cookie preferences (which categories you accepted) so we don't ask again on every visit. Persistent (until you clear browser storage)
nl_dismissed Remembers that you closed or completed the newsletter sign-up bar, so it doesn't appear again on later visits. Persistent (until you clear browser storage)

Because these entries are strictly necessary for the UI to behave correctly, they are exempt from the consent requirement under Romanian Law 506/2004 (transposing the ePrivacy Directive). We still describe them here for transparency.

4. Analytics (opt-in)

If you accept the Analytics category in the cookie banner, we load Google Analytics 4 (provided by Google Ireland Limited). Google Analytics helps us measure aggregated, anonymised usage of the Site — for example which pages are most visited, where traffic comes from, and how long visitors stay. We use this information only to improve the Site.

When Analytics is enabled, the following cookies may be set in your browser:

Cookie Purpose Duration
_ga Used by Google Analytics to distinguish unique users. 2 years
_ga_<ID> Used by Google Analytics 4 to persist session state. 2 years

We have configured Google Analytics with IP anonymisation and we do not use Google Signals, Google Ads linking, demographics, or any advertising features. Data is processed by Google in line with its Privacy Policy and Cookies overview. Google may transfer data outside the EEA under Standard Contractual Clauses.

You can withdraw your consent at any time via the Cookie Settings link in the footer. Once withdrawn, Google Analytics will stop loading on subsequent page views; existing cookies can be removed using your browser's storage tools.

5. What we do NOT use

  • No Google Ads, Facebook Pixel, TikTok Pixel or any advertising trackers.
  • No third-party social-media embeds that set tracking cookies.
  • No remarketing, profiling or cross-site behavioural advertising.
  • No analytics other than the optional Google Analytics described above.

If we ever introduce additional non-essential cookies, we will update this policy, add the relevant category to the banner, and ask for your explicit opt-in first.

6. Third-party services

Some pages contain links to third-party services (for example Kompostor for ticket purchases, or WhatsApp for direct chat). When you click those links and leave our site, the third party becomes the data controller and applies its own cookie and privacy policies.

7. How to control or delete cookies and browser storage

You have several options:

  • Change your consent — use the Cookie Settings link in the footer to update your category choices at any time.
  • Browser controls — most browsers let you block or delete cookies and localStorage from their settings:
  • Chrome / Edge — Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data.
  • Firefox — Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data.
  • Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data.
  • Mobile browsers — usually under Settings → Privacy or Storage.

Clearing storage will reset the cookie banner and the newsletter bar, so they may appear again.

8. Changes to this Policy

If we change which technologies the site uses, this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will change.
