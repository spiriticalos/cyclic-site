Our story

Born from a Love of the Underground.

Cyclic Agency was founded in 2009 by a group of friends who shared one obsession: authentic underground electronic music. At the time, Romania's scene was vibrant but fragmented — incredible local talent existed, but the infrastructure to consistently bring international artists and build sustainable events was lacking.

We set out to change that. Starting with intimate club nights in Bucharest, we quickly built a reputation for careful curation and exceptional experiences. The music always came first — we booked artists we genuinely believed in, not just names that would sell.

Over 17 years, we've expanded across 22 Romanian cities, produced over 500 events, and brought artists like Tale of Us, Dixon, DJ Koze, Nina Kraviz and Rodhad to Romanian audiences for the first time.

But we've never lost sight of what matters most: the connection between artists and audiences, the magic of a perfectly curated night, and the community that electronic music creates.

"Romania has one of the most passionate electronic music audiences in Europe. Our job is to honour that passion with events worthy of it."

Milestones

17 Years in Numbers

2009

Founded

Cyclic Agency launches in Bucharest with a series of intimate club nights at Control Club. The first events draw small but devoted crowds who share a passion for authentic underground music.

2012

First International Headliners

We bring our first major international acts to Romania, establishing Cyclic as the agency capable of delivering world-class electronic music events to Romanian audiences.

2015

Expansion to 10 Cities

Cyclic extends beyond Bucharest, producing events in Cluj-Napoca, Timișoara, Brașov, and 6 more cities. Romania's underground scene proves hungry for quality events everywhere, not just the capital.

2019

100th Event & Label Launch

We celebrate our 100th event with a special edition party in Bucharest. Cyclic Records is launched as our in-house imprint, giving our artist roster a home for their recorded music.

2026

22 Cities, 100+ Artists

Cyclic Agency now operates across 22 Romanian cities with a roster of over 100 artists. We've become the definitive home for underground electronic music events in Romania.

Our Values

Music First

Every decision starts with the music. We book artists we believe in. We design events around the sonic experience. Commercial considerations come second.

🤝

Community

Electronic music is nothing without its people. We build communities, support local artists, and create spaces where culture can genuinely grow.

✨

Authenticity

We don't chase trends. We stay true to underground music culture — its values, its aesthetics, its refusal to compromise for the mainstream.

Craft
