Frequently asked

DJ & mixer FAQ.

Do you have Pioneer CDJ-3000 for rental?
Yes, we rent out complete setups with Pioneer CDJ-3000 — the newest Pioneer players available for rental in Romania. Standard configuration: 4× CDJ-3000 + DJM-A9, Ethernet link between players, DJ booth monitors. Specific riders are met without exceptions.
Which DJ mixers do you have available?
Pioneer DJM-A9 (the 2024 flagship mixer), DJM-V10 for the riders that ask for it, Rane Seventy-Two for DJs who use Serato. Every mixer is checked before each event — faders, EQs and effects.
Do you also have turntables?
Yes. We have Technics SL-1200 / Pioneer PLX-1000 turntables for DJs who play vinyl or use the Rane Seventy-Two with time-code. Can be configured together as a hybrid CDJ + turntables booth.
How much does it cost to rent a Pioneer DJ booth?
The price depends on the configuration (2 vs 4 players), duration (a one-night event vs a multi-day festival), location and the services included (transport, setup, on-site tech). We send an itemised quote within 24h of receiving the brief.
Can I rent just the booth, without the sound system?
Yes, we offer dry hire for clients with their own sound system. Booth + DJ booth monitors, no main PA. Useful for clubs that already have a fixed system.
