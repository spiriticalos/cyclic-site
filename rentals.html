How it works

Our Process

01

Get in Touch

Contact us with your event details — date, location, expected capacity and type of event. We'll respond within 24 hours with initial availability and pricing information.

02

Technical Consultation

Our technical team will review your venue specifications and event requirements to propose the optimal equipment setup. We conduct site surveys for larger productions.

03

Custom Quote

Receive a detailed, transparent quote tailored to your specific needs. No hidden costs — we break down equipment, staffing, transport and any additional services clearly.

04

Event Day

Our crew arrives on schedule for setup and soundcheck. We stay for the duration of your event to handle any technical needs, and handle full breakdown and collection after.
