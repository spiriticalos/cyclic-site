Frequently asked

Lighting & truss FAQ.

What lighting do you rent out for events?
We have tour-grade inventory — FOS moving heads (12R, Movinghead 7R Hybrid), Prolights Halu LED bars and panels, FOS Pro D2 strobes, hazers and fog machines, ILDA-safe RGB lasers. Plus accessories: Chamsys console, dimming, DMX splitters.
Do you also have truss for the lighting?
Yes. Prolyte H30V lighting truss (club standard) and H40V (festival). Configurable in any shape — square, circle, triangular structures, with motors for flying. Load calculations are done by our rigger before every build.
Can I also rent lasers?
Yes, professional RGB lasers, compliant with ILDA and Romanian legislation. Power output between 3W and 30W per laser, depending on the event. We include a laser operator if real-time control is needed.
Does a lighting operator come with the equipment?
Yes, the standard package includes an LX operator for the Chamsys console. For long events (multi-day festivals), 2 operators in shifts. For dry hire (equipment only) we offer programming support.
Do you work outdoor events and festivals?
Yes. We have IP65 fixtures (water and dust resistant) for open-air. Prolyte H40V truss sized for wind load. We produced The Bohemian Society in the Danube Delta and events in extreme weather conditions.
