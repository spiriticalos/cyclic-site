Frequently asked

About Cyclic.

What is Cyclic Agency?
Cyclic Agency is a Romanian electronic music agency founded in 2009. It organizes house, techno and melodic events across 30 cities, handles artist booking and management, produces the festival The Bohemian Society, and rents professional event equipment through Cyclic Rentals.
What kind of events does Cyclic organize?
Electronic music events — house, techno, melodic techno and progressive — in clubs, on rooftops, in industrial spaces and open-air. From intimate club nights to multi-day festivals such as The Bohemian Society in the Danube Delta.
Does Cyclic bring international DJs and artists to Romania?
Yes. Over 17 years, Cyclic has brought world-class artists to Romanian stages, including Tale of Us, Dixon, Sasha, Nina Kraviz, Adriatique, Argy and Amelie Lens, and manages a roster of 100+ local and international artists such as Optick, Mihai Popoviciu and Adrian Eftimie.
Does Cyclic rent equipment for events?
Yes, through Cyclic Rentals. It rents professional sound systems (Void Acoustics, d&b audiotechnik, L-Acoustics), DJ equipment (Pioneer CDJ-3000, DJM-A9) and lighting with rigging (Robe, Martin, Ayrton moving heads, Prolyte truss) to promoters, companies and artists across Romania.
Which cities does Cyclic operate in?
Cyclic produces events in 30 Romanian cities, headquartered in Bucharest with a constant presence in Cluj-Napoca, Iași, Timișoara, Constanța, Focșani and Sfântu Gheorghe (Danube Delta), home of the The Bohemian Society festival.
How do I contact Cyclic for an event or booking?
For events and artist booking: Ionuț Hupcă, Director of Events — ionut@cyclic.ro, +40 721 381 922. For equipment rental, each rental page has a quote request form with a reply within 24 hours from a production manager.
